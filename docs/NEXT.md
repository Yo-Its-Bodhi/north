## Active outcome

Complete the workout-planning and tracking foundation before real-world validation.

Use the complete loop:

1. Select any strength day in the weekly plan.
2. Edit its own exercise order, sets, target, load, and rest.
3. Find movements through search, body-area, and equipment filters.
4. Start that planned workout without affecting another day.
5. Save it against the correct planned day.
6. Find and correct it later through complete workout history.

## Done when

- Every strength day preserves a distinct workout.
- Exercise discovery works across hundreds of movements without a long manual scroll.
- Completed workouts remain searchable and editable.
- The next bounded outcome is ready for gym-floor validation.
