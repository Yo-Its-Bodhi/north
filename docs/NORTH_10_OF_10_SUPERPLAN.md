# North 10/10 superplan

## Product standard

North should feel athletic, warm, confident and alive. It must never resemble accounting software, a crypto dashboard, or an indiscriminate rainbow. The visual foundation uses darker warm-neutral backgrounds, controlled North blue, and restrained activity accents. Colour communicates purpose; it is not decoration.

All work remains local until the complete overhaul has passed the release gate in Item 15.

## Build order

1. **Professional anatomy system - complete locally.** Reusable front/rear figures now provide named, tappable regions; data-driven primary/secondary/supporting roles; specific chest, deltoid, arm, torso, hip, thigh and lower-leg anatomy; compact Training and detailed exercise variants; accessible labels; responsive light/dark presentation; and tested movement-family mappings.
2. **Whole-product visual overhaul - active.** One surface, type, icon, spacing, border and motion system across entry, Today, Training, Journey, Nova, You, workout, results and admin. The member app now uses its warm darker-neutral foundation, athletic condensed hierarchy and restrained teal/green/amber/violet semantic accents instead of stark white, excessive blue and rainbow gradients. Screen-by-screen refinement continues.
3. **Training dashboard - complete locally.** The approved hierarchy is restored: header and calendar, weekly rhythm, Nova call, selected-day hero, live anatomy, immediate Start/View actions, visible multiple-session stack, compact recent sessions and weekly performance. Editing remains available without dominating the dashboard, while quick logs, programs, library and imports live inside one secondary tools drawer.
4. **Best-in-class recorder - complete locally.** The gym-floor recorder now provides prefilled planned reps, previous exercise and set results, large one-tap completion, fast load/repetition steppers, add/remove sets, honest incomplete-set handling, elapsed time, adjustable automatic rest, wake lock and haptic feedback, live equipment alternatives, notes, pass-and-return flow, sticky one-handed actions, synchronous page-hide preservation, offline/reload recovery, resumable activity drafts and unlimited activity history. Multiple strength and endurance sessions can coexist on the same day and submit into permanent North records.
5. **Exercise profiles.** Credible demonstrations, anatomy, personal history, technique, common errors, breathing, setup, safety and alternatives; premium coverage begins with the most-used movement families.
6. **Workout edit station and routine builder - complete locally.** Premade and My Workouts now share one searchable core destination with favourites, recent routines, full custom building, searchable body-area/equipment exercise selection, multi-equipment controls, prescription editing, reorder/delete, validation, autosave, scheduling, immediate preparation and in-session alternatives. Today-only/permanent substitution controls remain deliberately attached to preparation and gym-floor editing where their scope is clear.
7. **Meaningful Journey history.** An unbounded training calendar/timeline for completed workouts, substantial activities, PRs, milestones, reflections and corrections. Low-value incidental health records do not pollute the story.
8. **Useful Nova.** Record-aware conversation, evidence, confidence, safe plan proposals, progression, substitutions, memory controls and no silent changes.
9. **Premium first run.** Soft logo load, excellent account/recovery flow, resumable onboarding, editable first week, equipment multi-select and immediate first useful action.
10. **Wearables later.** Samsung/Health Connect reliability and incremental background sync resume only after the core experience is excellent.
11. **You versus Account.** You presents identity, direction, progress and learned traits. Account, devices, privacy, services, data and installation move behind a top-banner profile control. Body-weight, lifting-weight and distance units are independent.
12. **Serious owner control.** Complete the druwbi console for people, codes, content, operations, health diagnostics, audit, safety and emergency controls.
13. **Personality and celebration - complete locally.** The result experience now recognises evidence-backed PRs, first workouts, comeback sessions, multi-week rhythms and completed program weeks with distinct athletic treatments, gentle haptics and reduced-motion parity. It celebrates the return rather than shaming the gap, and never invents an achievement North cannot prove.
14. **Real-device quality - complete locally.** North now has audited 320/375/390/430 phone, 768/1024 tablet and wide-desktop layouts; safe-area and landscape handling; coarse-pointer touch targets; sticky one-handed workout completion controls; standalone PWA metadata and shortcuts; versioned offline navigation fallback; explicit offline state; durable interrupted-workout recovery; route focus and skip navigation; named controls; large-text, high-contrast and comprehensive reduced-motion behavior; four rendered visual baselines; and nine automated browser journeys covering navigation, planning, library, Nova, keyboard access, responsive overflow, offline use, reload recovery and onboarding.
15. **Release only when finished.** Staging, security/privacy, performance, update safety, backup/restore, monitoring, release checklist and rollback; then deploy the completed overhaul.

## Deliberately deferred

Nutrition logging, public social feeds, marketplaces, elaborate 3D anatomy, Apple Health, and massive low-quality media expansion remain deferred until the core earns them.
