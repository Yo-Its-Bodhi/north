## Recovered decisions not yet fully captured

### Profile becomes You

The fifth destination evolved from **Profile**, through **Identity**, to
**You**. It is not a social profile or a settings dashboard. It makes the
relationship visible by answering:

> Who has North learned I am becoming?

### The Journey Mark

Use a personal Journey Mark instead of a profile photo. It acts like a private
passport or compass and may become richer over time. It is not a public badge,
level, or popularity mechanic.

### What North has learned

The preferred language is **What North has learned**, not “North currently
knows.” The wording is humbler and makes continued learning explicit.

Learned traits should be earned from logs, conversations, and patterns—not
entered as profile fields. Every learned trait must be explainable. For example:

> Over the last eight weeks you chose cycling most often on Mondays and Fridays,
> and usually reported more energy afterward.

The purpose is not merely to remember. It is to help the traveller understand
themselves.

### Conversational onboarding

Onboarding should eventually feel like a first conversation with Nova, not a
long questionnaire. North can ask only what it needs now, then learn naturally
over the following days. The exact onboarding conversation remains future work.

### Companion language

Prefer **Together for 143 days** over “Member since.” Quiet relationship and
journey language is more North than account or subscription language.

### You page restraint

The emerging structure was:

1. Journey Mark and identity
2. Your Direction as a short mission statement
3. What North has learned, with evidence on demand
4. A small number of earned identity/pride markers
5. Quiet personalisation and account controls

Avoid profile-completion percentages, vanity statistics, follower mechanics,
and card-heavy layouts.

## Lessons proven by Anchored/Jay

Anchored/Jay remains a separate product, but its gym-floor testing validated:

- Pass for now and Return to
- Constant autosave and safe resume
- Searchable, categorised exercise selection
- Custom exercises
- Previous performance and coaching cues
- Expandable On deck context
- Morning/Night contrast rules
- Weekly plans, coach import, and ad-hoc rides as later workflow needs

North may adopt proven interaction patterns without importing Jay’s identity,
repository structure, or product scope.

## Current implementation consequence

The next North increment is to replace the hard-coded demonstration workout
with real workout preparation: searchable exercise selection, custom movements,
removal, reordering, and persistence. This strengthens the agreed MVP loop and
does not prematurely build the deferred You page.
