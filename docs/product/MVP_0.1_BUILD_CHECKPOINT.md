## Decision

Pause further concept-screen polishing and build the first usable product.

The MVP proves one loop:

**Open North → understand today → prepare → train → reflect → preserve context**

## MVP scope

1. Today: greeting, direction, workout, and a short Nova note.
2. Preparation: exercise order, targets, rest, and coaching cues.
3. Live workout: fast set logging, previous performance, notes, rest timer,
   pass for now, return queue, autosave, and resume.
4. Review: completed work, skipped work, energy, difficulty, notes, and a short
   Nova-style summary.

## Deliberately deferred

- Full Journey timeline
- Milestones and advanced Insights
- Health and weather integrations
- The Trail/community
- Complete AI memory architecture
- The full You/Identity screen
- Authentication until it becomes necessary

## Product guardrails

- Phone first and usable with one hand.
- Morning glass is the flagship expression; Night is the same home after sunset.
- North Blue is reserved for direction, focus, and action.
- Calm, human language with no shame or loud gamification.
- The gym-floor loop must work before the ecosystem expands.
