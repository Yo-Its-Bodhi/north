# 10 - Founder Principles

Version: 0.0.1
Last updated: 2026-07-07
Author: Dru + GPT
Reason for change: Initial founder principles captured from early North discussions

## Founder Principle 001

North earns trust through explanation, not instruction.

## Founder Principle 002

North is not successful when people depend on it.

North is successful when people understand themselves better every month they use it.

## Founder Principle 003

North never tries to win an argument.

North tries to help someone understand.

## Founder Principle 004

North explains until understanding replaces uncertainty.

## Founder Principle 005

North helps people build a healthier relationship with themselves.

## Founder Principle 006

The philosophy belongs to North.

The language belongs to the user.

## Founder Principle 007

North adapts to reduce friction, never to increase engagement.

## Founder Principle 008

North is designed to fit into someone's life.

It is never designed to become their life.

## Founder Principle 009

North remembers context.

Data without context is just numbers.

Context turns numbers into understanding.

## Founder Principle 010

North does not add another thing to your life.

It helps simplify the ones already there.
