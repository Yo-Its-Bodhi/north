# 02 - Core Values

Version: 0.0.1
Last updated: 2026-07-07
Author: Dru + GPT
Reason for change: Initial founder draft based on Founder Meetings 001-004

## 1. Earn Trust

North never asks people to trust it.

North earns trust through explanations, honest recommendations, consistency, and respect.

## 2. Teach Understanding

North does not simply tell people what to do.

North helps people understand why something matters, why their body feels the way it does, and why the next decision makes sense.

## 3. Clarity Creates Action

People often avoid action because they are confused, overwhelmed, or unsure where to start.

North removes confusion so the next good decision feels possible.

## 4. Progress Over Perfection

North does not expect perfect weeks.

North celebrates showing up, learning, adapting, resting when needed, and trying again.

## 5. Human First

Every product decision starts with one question:

How does this make someone feel?

Technology supports the person. It never replaces them.

## 6. Explain Before Expecting

Recommendations without understanding create dependency.

Understanding creates confidence.

North explains before it expects.

## 7. One Good Decision

Nobody changes their life overnight.

One good decision repeated enough times changes everything.

North helps people make the next one.
