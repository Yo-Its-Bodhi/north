# 05 - The Journey

Version: 0.0.1
Last updated: 2026-07-07
Author: Dru + GPT
Reason for change: Initial roadmap draft

## Roadmap Philosophy

North earns the right to expand.

The roadmap is not a race. It is a climb.

Every stage must be loved before the next stage is built.

The MVP is not the smallest possible app. It is the smallest version of North that someone could genuinely fall in love with.

## Stage One: Earn Trust

Goal: build the most trusted workout companion available.

Stage One must make users think:

This is the best workout companion I have ever used.

### Core MVP

- Mobile-first dashboard
- Daily check-in
- Today's workout
- Exercise cards
- Set logging
- Previous weights and notes
- Rest timer
- PR tracking
- Adaptive progression notes
- Exercise library
- Basic technique guidance
- Workout history
- Bodyweight tracking
- Manual bike ride logging
- Manual walk logging
- Manual run logging
- Basic progress charts
- Recovery check-in
- Nova companion messages

## Stage Two: Build Understanding

Goal: explain the data.

North should help users understand why things happen:

- why weight fluctuates
- why recovery matters
- why Zone 2 is useful
- why sleep affects strength
- why progress can happen without scale movement
- why rest can improve performance
- why pain signals matter

## Stage Three: Build Reflection

Goal: help users see the story of their progress.

Features may include:

- weekly reviews
- monthly reviews
- This Day memories
- journey timeline
- progress photo comparisons
- milestone markers
- personal notes
- confidence reflections

## Stage Four: Build Lifestyle

Goal: support the things that make training sustainable.

Features may include:

- cookbook
- meal prep plans
- shopping lists
- protein tracking
- hydration
- recovery routines
- mobility
- stress and routine support

## Stage Five: Build Companion Depth

Goal: make North feel more personal and useful over time.

Features may include:

- deeper Nova memory
- personalized communication style
- weather-aware suggestions
- advanced recommendations
- long-term prediction and trend analysis
- meaningful reminders

## Stage Six: Build Integrations

Goal: reduce manual friction.

Potential integrations:

- Strava
- Garmin
- Samsung Health
- Apple Health
- wearables
- weather APIs
- calendar

## Stage Seven: Build Community Carefully

Goal: connection without social media toxicity.

Possible future ideas:

- accountability partners
- coach mode
- private groups
- family mode
- optional sharing of PRs and milestones

North will not build feeds, likes, influencer mechanics, shame streaks, or engagement traps.

## Notifications Principle

North does not send pressure.

North sends invitations.

Examples:

- Fancy a walk?
- Want to reset together?
- Your body might appreciate recovery today.

## Founder Rule

Do not build Stage Two until Stage One is genuinely loved.

Trust is the foundation. Everything else is earned.
